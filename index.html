At our core is a revolutionary approach to biological sensing. By using entangled quantum particles as remote, high-fidelity sensors, we can record the spatiotemporal pathology generated by a system of antibodies or the electrochemical patterns of neural pathways. This technology translates cognition in real time.

  • Microenvironment Dynamics: Tracks subtle cellular deviations influencing chemical signaling, enabling predictive analysis of disease progression and drug efficacy.
