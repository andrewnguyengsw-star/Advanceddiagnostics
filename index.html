From Molecules to Medicine

We bridge molecular biology and clinical insights using entangled qubits as biological sensors. For the first time, we can track the precise location and activity of biomarkers in the body.

This dynamic map allows unprecedented understanding of disease progression, guiding precise interventions.

How It Works

Quantum Entanglement

Biomarkers are tagged with entangled qubits, allowing one to sense biological events while the other is monitored remotely.

Real-Time Interaction

Biological events, like antibody binding, instantly affect the quantum state of the probe, capturing activity in real time.

Instantaneous Mapping

Entanglement ensures changes are mirrored to the monitoring qubit, creating a precise map of biological activity.
