How It Works

AECION bridges biology and computation — using quantum-entangled photons to create a live, digital twin of the body. Here’s how we turn molecular events into real-time insight.

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1 – Biological Trigger

An autoantibody binds to its target in the body, creating a cascade of biological events. This triggers a neural impulse — a rapid change in electrical charge across neurons.

Step 2 – Quantum Coupling

A signal photon is quantum-coupled to this neural event. The photon's state is altered by the body’s electrical change.

Step 3 – Entanglement Transfer

The idler photon instantly reflects this change, enabling immediate monitoring without classical signals.

Step 4 – Digital Twin Readout

The digital twin translates quantum state shifts into readable signals for real-time diagnostics.
