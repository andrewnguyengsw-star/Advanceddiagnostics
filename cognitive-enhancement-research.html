Decoding the Blueprint of Human Intelligence

We are leveraging cutting-edge neuroscience to unlock the potential of the human mind and pioneer the next generation of cognitive enhancement.

Our Technology

Cognitive enhancement research is powered by advanced technology that can trace individual neural impulses and translate their encoded messages. This allows us an unprecedented view into the mechanics of thought.

By correlating IQ test outcomes with specific changes in brain activity, we uncovered critical neural connections underlying high intellect. Observing how individual neural connections drive changes in IQ performance allowed us to identify key circuits that differentiate high and low intellectual function.

Our Method

Our science models the brain activity of individual test takers relative to their score, tracing each neuron that fires over the entire duration of the IQ test. By observing these changes across space and time, we can reasonably infer the neuroanatomical basis of intelligence.

Applications of Our Discoveries

These findings have become the central focus of our cognitive enhancement research, driving two key areas of innovation.

Neural Biomarkers for IQ

The identified neural connections serve as precise biomarkers that can be targeted and measured, allowing us to develop and validate interventions designed to enhance IQ.

A Template for Smart Drugs

The specific activity patterns of high intellect serve as a functional template, guiding the rational design and development of future smart drugs with unprecedented precision.

Our Future Pipeline

These insights will become the targets of our smart drug pipeline. Our ultimate goal is to leverage this knowledge to create a true smart drug that enhances human cognitive potential.
