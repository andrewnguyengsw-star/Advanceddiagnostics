Project Seer

Conceptual Framework & Military Applications for advanced predictive capabilities across tactical, operational, and strategic levels.

Explore Project Seer

Overview

Project Seer is an initiative exploring the potential for soldiers and decision-makers to leverage advanced predictive capabilities ranging from tactical precognition to long-horizon forecasting — merging human cognition with predictive and quantum-inspired models to gain decisive advantage on the battlefield, while maintaining ethical, legal, and strategic safeguards.

Capability Vision

Tactical Precognition

Short-horizon capabilities spanning seconds to minutes. Improves reaction time, survivability, and real-time decision-making for individual soldiers and squads.

Operational Foresight

Mid-horizon capabilities spanning minutes to hours. Supports commanders in forecasting enemy maneuvers, logistics bottlenecks, and engagement outcomes.

Strategic Prediction

Long-horizon capabilities spanning days to months. Assists in identifying emerging threats, campaign inflection points, and geopolitical risks.

Military Application Areas

  • Situational Awareness: Early perception of threats.
  • Command and Control: Predictive overlays for enemy courses of action.
  • Force Protection: Anticipating hazards to reduce casualties.
  • ISR Prioritization: Directing surveillance assets.
  • Targeting and Effects: Identifying optimal engagement timing.
  • Cyber & Electronic Warfare Defense: Preemptive rerouting and defense.
  • Logistics and Sustainment: Forecasting supply bottlenecks.
  • Training and Readiness: Enhancing decision-making simulations.
  • Psychological Operations: Anticipating population behavior.
  • Human Performance Management: Predicting fatigue and cognitive overload.

Safeguards, Risks & Governance

Safeguards & Human Oversight

Strict safeguards including human-in-the-loop control, transparency, and operator fail-safes ensure lawful and responsible use of Project Seer.

Risks

  • Automation bias and false signals.
  • Adversary manipulation of predictive systems.
  • Psychological harm from “prophetic” scenarios.
  • Escalation risks and legal exposure.
  • Security threats to predictive systems.

Governance & Verification

Governance includes validation, red-teaming, audit logs, oversight boards, staged deployments, and security controls to ensure safe use.

Roadmap & Recommendations

Advancing Project Seer requires careful planning, including defining mission-limited use-cases, establishing ethics frameworks, conducting rigorous simulations, piloting with oversight, and scaling cautiously.
