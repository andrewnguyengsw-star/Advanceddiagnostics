Inside Project Atlas™

Project Atlas™ explores a novel tactile information-extraction human augmentation, where touch becomes a multidimensional interface to decode structured physical and cognitive data embedded in objects and environments.

Tactis™ transforms the skin into a sensor array enhanced by quantum coherence, enabling humans to perceive numbers, text, images, memories, human experiences, scientific data, and behavioral patterns without reliance on vision.

Fingertips act as transducers, converting encoded signals into neural patterns interpretable by the brain or external processors, while tunable sensory fields detect photonic echoes, thermal signatures, or electromagnetic imprints left by objects.
