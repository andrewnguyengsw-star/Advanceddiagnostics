Our Quantum Application

Observing biology in real time at the quantum level — from molecules to entire human systems.

1. The Challenge of Scale: Capturing the Anti-Jo-1 Antigen

Our quantum platform tackles one of the hardest problems in biomedicine: understanding the histidyl-tRNA synthetase (HisRS) protein — the target of Anti-Jo-1 antibodies.

  • Massive Complexity: HisRS contains 6,000–11,000 atoms, making it too complex for classical simulation.
  • Beyond Classical Simulation: Accurately modeling folding, interactions, and dynamics requires solving the quantum behavior of every electron and nucleus — beyond even the fastest supercomputers.
  • Beyond Current Quantum Limits: NISQ-era quantum computers can only handle very small molecules. Simulating HisRS at biological scale would normally require millions of error-corrected qubits.

Our application overcomes this by using quantum sensing and entanglement rather than brute-force simulation — extracting real-world, real-time data directly from the biological system.

2. The Method of Observation: Quantum Sensing of Disease in Action

Instead of simulating molecules in silico, our approach observes biology as it happens — turning living molecular systems into quantum-readable data sources.

Quantum Sensing Network

A distributed array of quantum probes interfaces with the biological environment containing HisRS.

Entangled Measurement

Probes are quantum-entangled, so a perturbation at one point in the protein is correlated across the network, providing a synchronized view of molecular dynamics.

Ultra-Sensitive Coherence Tracking

Each probe’s quantum state is exquisitely sensitive to local electromagnetic fluctuations caused by protein folding, domain motions, and molecular interactions.

4D Pathology Mapping

By measuring changes in probe coherence, we reconstruct a spatiotemporal map of HisRS behavior — watching in real time how misfolding triggers the autoimmune cascade of antisynthetase syndrome.

3. What This Enables

  • Quantum Simulation at Biological Scale: Understanding highly complex molecules without needing impractically large quantum computers.
  • Quantum Metrology for Medicine: Non-invasive, real-time observation of molecular pathology.
  • Paradigm Shift: Instead of static pictures and population statistics, we observe the quantum-level events that initiate disease, enabling precisely targeted interventions.

Observing Disease Through Entanglement

At the heart of our platform is quantum entanglement as a measurement tool for autoimmune-driven pathology.

  • Biological Trigger: Elevated Anti-Jo-1 antibodies bind HisRS, triggering tissue damage and inflammatory cascades.
  • Electrochemical Signaling: Field changes transduced through peripheral and central nervous system alter polarization gradients.
  • Quantum Coupling: Entangled photon pairs detect these subtle polarization shifts and carry information to a remote quantum readout.
  • Real-Time Observation: Measurements reflect collective molecular and electrochemical dynamics at concentrations typical of antisynthetase syndrome.

Universal Quantum Biosensing Application

Our platform can capture any human biological state — from single-molecule interactions to whole-body signaling — in real time.

  • Beyond Autoimmunity: Autoimmune diseases, cancer, neurodegeneration, infectious disease, metabolic/hormonal regulation, and drug-target interactions.
  • How It Works: Quantum sensing network, signal coupling, entangled readout, and spatiotemporal reconstruction.
  • Why This is Different: Observes biology directly rather than simulating it — bypassing the need for massive fault-tolerant qubits.

How This Compares to Quantum Computing

Quantum Computing

  • Goal: Simulate molecules or run general-purpose algorithms.
  • Requires millions of error-corrected qubits for large proteins.
  • Produces theoretical predictions.
  • Mainly for future drug discovery or optimization problems.
  • Still many years from simulating full biological systems at scale.

Our Quantum Biosensing Platform

  • Goal: Directly observe and analyze every human condition and every drug in real time.
  • Works with quantum probes and entangled photons — no large-scale fault-tolerant qubits required.
  • Produces real-world, real-time measurements of immune, neural, and metabolic activity.
  • Analyzes every molecule, pathway, and intervention dynamically.
  • Applicable to any disease, biological signal, or human condition, with clinically relevant readouts.
