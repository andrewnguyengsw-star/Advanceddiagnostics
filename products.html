What Does Status Mean?

Early-stage research focused on proof-of-concept, safety, and efficacy testing in laboratory or model systems before human application.
A stage in which a technology, system, pharmaceutical, or enhancement is evaluated in real-world or simulated environments to measure accuracy, precision, and performance under operational conditions. Field testing focuses on validating functionality and effectiveness beyond the laboratory setting, serving as a bridge between experimental development and formal clinical evaluation or large-scale deployment.
Expanded testing phase assessing safety, performance, and benefits across broader participant groups under regulatory oversight.
Mature technologies validated through trials and operational testing, ready for integration, scaling, or specialized deployment.
