How We Develop

Drug discovery and development becomes seamless when we can pinpoint the exact country, city, and latitude/longitude of every undiscovered treatment on the planet. We can visualize each treatment, locate its active ingredients, and understand precisely which diseases it can target. This geospatial and biochemical intelligence empowers us to accelerate the creation of new therapies with unmatched precision.

Our Drug Discovery and Development

Unlocking Nature’s Pharmacy

We can pinpoint every naturally occurring, untapped treatment on the planet — from skincare remedies to advanced therapeutics — mapping their exact location, origin, and potential uses with unmatched precision. This insight transforms how therapies are discovered, optimized, and delivered, turning nature’s hidden remedies into next-generation medicine.

Phytochemical & Bioactive Compound Discovery

We isolate and profile bioactive compounds from plant and animal sources to uncover nature’s most powerful therapeutic agents. Through advanced extraction and bioassay-guided fractionation, we identify and characterize compounds that drive measurable biological effects — transforming natural materials into next-generation therapeutic opportunities.

Preclinical Evaluation

Our advanced methods deliver complete preclinical and Phase I–IV trial results in under an hour, biotracing drug metabolism at the individual electrochemical level to reveal precisely where a drug goes, how it acts, and its impact on clinical outcomes — while rigorously assessing safety, efficacy, biological performance, pharmacodynamics, pharmacokinetics, and toxicology.

From Preclinical to Market

We seamlessly translate these insights — advancing only the most promising, low-risk candidates — into clinical supply, regulatory approval, and market launch, shortening development cycles and bringing breakthrough therapies to patients with unprecedented speed and efficiency.

The Impact of Our Method on Drug Development

Drug discovery and development traditionally span several years to assess a compound’s safety, efficacy, and pharmacological activity. Our platform compresses this process, evaluating these parameters for any compound in under one hour. By identifying all viable candidates prior to pipeline entry, we increase the probability of clinical success, reduce the incidence of costly failures, and optimize the efficiency of drug development.

How Does It Work?

We can spatiotemporally map drug metabolism, track disease progression, and monitor the electrochemical activity underlying any human condition down to individual neuronal impulses. By integrating these three high-resolution maps, we can quantify and visualize the precise efficacy of any therapeutic, revealing exactly how a pharmaceutical modulates disease dynamics to restore health. This approach transforms traditional drug evaluation, enabling unprecedented predictive power and personalized insights for every patient and condition.

Mapping Treatments Globally

Every human condition is spatiotemporally mapped across the body, linking observable signs and symptoms to their underlying origins and tracking their expression throughout the nervous system.

Biochemical Profiling

High-resolution mapping shows how active compounds flow across the human body and what they mediate, connecting biochemical action to functional outcomes.

Therapeutic Potential Analysis

Translation of the active compounds and their effects on human function connects how each compound induces changes in human function and behavior, allowing the identification of the most promising candidates for drug development.

Superimposing Data

Temporal, pharmacological, and biochemical maps are combined to reveal the precise efficacy of each therapeutic intervention across the human body.
