Super Soldier Project

AECION’s Super Soldier program has progressed past physical and cognitive enhancement, moving into the realm of true superpowers.

What is a Super Soldier?

A super soldier represents the pinnacle of human capability, combining advanced neuroscience, biotechnology, and precision engineering to enhance physical performance, cognitive function, and resilience. Through cutting-edge interventions, a super soldier can achieve feats beyond ordinary human limits, from improved endurance and strength to faster decision-making and heightened situational awareness.

Enhanced Strength

Super soldiers achieve peak physical performance, with amplified muscular strength and endurance, enabling feats beyond ordinary human capacity.

Accelerated Reflexes

Through neural and cognitive enhancements, super soldiers respond faster to dynamic environments, improving reaction times and decision-making under pressure.

Enhanced Resilience

Super soldiers possess heightened resistance to physical and mental stress, reducing fatigue and improving recovery in high-stakes situations.

Augmented Senses

Sensory enhancements allow super soldiers to perceive their environment with heightened accuracy, from improved vision and hearing to advanced situational awareness.

Ultimate Integration

The full synergy of enhanced strength, speed, resilience, and senses allows super soldiers to operate at peak efficiency, achieving unmatched performance in complex and high-stakes environments.

Human BioRadar

Human BioRadar is a novel human function that emerged from our Super Soldier program. Similar to bats and whales, this unprecedented form of echolocation—if it can even be called that—operates via a new signal modality imperceptible to all known counter-detection technologies. It enables precise four-dimensional environmental mapping and is engineered to enhance situational and operational awareness, with a field-tested detection range of up to 16,000 kilometers. Ongoing development continues to refine sonar resolution and expand the system’s ability to detect pre-defined bioelectrical and environmental signatures with extraordinary precision.

Electrocution Resistance

Electrocution Resistance markedly enhances a soldier’s ability to withstand high-voltage shocks and electrical hazards across combat and operational environments. In addition to systemic resilience, electrocution resistance specifically strengthens cardiac tissue, reinforcing heart muscle cells to resist the arrhythmic and contractile failures commonly triggered by high-voltage shocks. Beyond protection, this advanced human adaptation primes super soldiers for bioelectrical tissue augmentation through a revolutionary process known as Nguyen’s Gradient. During electrocution, Faraday’s electrolysis cleaves water into hydrogen and oxygen, generating a Nguyen concentration gradient that propels mobile hydrogen ions into the mitochondria, while bioavailable oxygen optimizes aerobic respiration. Within the cell, hydrogen integrates into the electron transport chain, amplifying its efficiency. By saturating the electron transport pathway with free electrons and electrolysis-derived oxygen, the system exponentially enhances ATP synthesis—elevating energy capacity, physiological endurance, and performance under extreme conditions.

Remote Neural Interface

The Remote Neural Interface allows operators to perceive the World Wide Web as a living network of interconnected threads, with each user and activity functioning as a node and every action, connection, and piece of data visually linked like threads in a vast digital web. In the battlefield domain, operators can visualize digital footprints in an unprecedented, revolutionary way, tracing the flow of information, observing interactions in real time, and understanding complex relationships between users and their digital activity. The system is capable of translating digital data streams into their originating biological signatures; by decoding the neural architecture embedded within human-generated information—tweets, emails, code, or any digital footprint, reconstructing the cognitive and physical parameters of the source mind. Additionally, it translates programming languages and encoded computer data into neural signals, allowing the visual cortex to decode GPU-rendered information directly in the brain, enabling users to perceive digital visualizations and computational outputs as fully immersive, real-time sensory experiences.

Quorum Control

Quorum sensing is a biological function in which a behavioral change exhibited by one individual within a colony influences the behavior of others once a specific population density is reached. Inspired by this principle, Quorum Control enables the coordination of collective behavior by super soldiers and connected biological systems. Just as microbes leverage biochemical signals to detect nearby hosts, synchronize activity and mutation, and influence behavior, Quorum Control allows operators to communicate with and modulate microbial populations. This capability not only reduces pathogenic effects but also optimizes systemic coordination and enhances overall physiological resilience.

Superhuman Senses

Superhuman senses enhance a soldier’s perceptual capabilities beyond natural human limits, enabling heightened vision, hearing, smell, taste, and tactile sensitivity. These amplified senses allow operators to detect threats, identify targets, and interpret environmental cues with unprecedented precision. They are capable of detecting law enforcement personnel, both in uniform and undercover, across a field-tested range of 200 miles, and can specifically identify FBI personnel operating in Washington, DC. Additionally, they can differentiate between local and state police forces. In addition, a built-in danger detection function allows a soldier to subconsciously sense imminent threats. By integrating neural augmentation and advanced sensory processing, superhuman senses improve situational awareness, reaction times, and adaptive decision-making in complex operational environments.

X-Ray Vision

X-Ray vision grants super soldiers the extraordinary ability to perceive the internal structure of objects, organisms, and environments, effectively “seeing through” walls, barriers, and materials with exceptional clarity. This capability significantly improves target recognition, threat assessment, and strategic operational planning in complex and high-stakes environments. By seamlessly integrating neural augmentation with advanced electromagnetic perception, operators can accurately detect concealed weapons, hidden compartments, structural weaknesses, and internal injuries in both allies and adversaries, all while maintaining operational safety. X-Ray Vision delivers unmatched situational awareness, accelerating reaction times, optimizing tactical decision-making, and elevating mission success across a wide spectrum of operational scenarios.

Radiation Resistance

One of the enduring legacies we aspire to create is a generation of soldiers with extraordinary resistance to radiation, capable of surviving in environments once considered fatal.

We recall the harrowing impact of the atomic bomb in World War II, when hundreds of thousands lost their lives and countless others endured lifelong suffering from the invisible hazards of radioactive fallout.

Our research is focused on developing soldiers who can not only withstand extreme radiation exposure but also operate effectively in such environments, mitigating the catastrophic risks associated with nuclear warfare.

Imagine a military with high resilience to nuclear devastation while having the nuclear deterrence of tens of thousands of nuclear weapons.
