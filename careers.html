Careers at AECION

Join the teams designing tomorrow’s breakthroughs today.

Join the Architects of Tomorrow

At AECION, we don’t wait for the future — we build it. Every breakthrough we achieve is powered by minds unafraid to explore what others call impossible.

From quantum systems and neurobiology to adaptive AI and synthetic biology, our teams operate at the edge of discovery. We invite engineers, scientists, and visionaries who see challenges as invitations to innovate.

What Type of Company You’ll Be Joining

Scientific Frontier

At AECION, you won’t be studying molecules and organisms alone — you’ll explore disease as it unfolds across space and time. Our technology maps every condition at the level of individual neuronal impulses, revealing every biomarker and therapeutic target with unmatched precision. Here, you’ll work at the frontier of science, uncovering the unknown and helping redefine what’s possible in medicine and human potential.

Mapping Human Behavior

We can biotrace every human behavior down to the level of individual neuronal impulses, revealing how subtle variations shape outcomes. By mapping the architecture of human consciousness itself, we illuminate the patterns that define thought, action, and potential. Here at AECION, you’ll explore the deepest frontiers of human awareness, satisfy your curiosity, and pursue work that could define your life’s purpose.

Your Discoveries

Your effort and dedication are your own. At AECION, the accolades you earn through your discoveries belong solely to you. We provide the insights and tools — so you can shine on your own two feet and make your mark on the frontiers of science. It is likely that many of our team members will become Nobel Prize winners in the future.

Uncharted

While our black technology department tackles projects that seem alien, at AECION you’ll work with scientific theories and principles that won’t exist for hundreds or even thousands of years. You’ll apply methods far beyond contemporary understanding, pushing the frontier of discovery and redefining the very boundaries of what science can achieve.
