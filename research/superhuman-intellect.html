A Generative Leap in Intelligence

How does our understanding of intelligence change in the year 2200+?

1. IQ in 2025

IQ measures a person’s aptitude, which ultimately arises from neural activity. Differences in neural processing—such as sensory perception, recognition, and circuit-based problem solving—underlie the variation seen across test takers and produce the IQ distribution.

2. IQ in 2125+

Although IQ stems from nature and nurture, variations in individual neural impulses account for a significant portion of test outcomes. The combined activity of these impulses underlies the performance measured by the test. In this sense, discrete neural circuits can be seen as the foundation of high intellect.

3. IQ in 2200+

Once all neural circuits underlying intellect are mapped, mimicking the activity patterns of high-IQ individuals—via targeted modulation or therapeutics—could enhance cognitive ability. This represents the first end stage of superintelligence, where humans achieve the maximum intelligence allowed by the current evolutionary state.

At this level, a mind comparable to Newton, Einstein, Gauss, or Darwin—a revolutionary intellect capable of paradigm-shifting impact—could be achieved through pharmaceutical augmentation.

The Evolutionary End State

How our understanding of intelligence culminates in the year 3025+

1. IQ in 2500 A.D.

By the year 2500, new challenges will arise, including optimizing processing power and brain efficiency—factors constrained by brain mass and volume—alongside other discoveries that advance our understanding of the brain. The key breakthrough will be learning how to enhance efficiency and processing power within these biological limits.

2. IQ in 2800 A.D.

In the year 2800, discovery of novel neural circuits will yield the breakthrough that will allow humans to push past normal human limitations. This discovery marks the beginning of the second major breakthrough, where human intelligence begins advancing once again.

3. IQ in 3200+

The complete elucidation of the brain's architecture and mechanisms will not just trigger a paradigm shift—it will end the paradigm of unguided evolution. In this advanced technological epoch, the ability to precisely control neural impulses, and thereby orchestrate collective cognition, becomes the new foundation of reality. This is the threshold of the end state.

The proof lies in the path we took. We unraveled human consciousness, not as a spirit, but as a mechanism. We systematically mapped the neural correlates for the full spectrum of human experience—from the pathology of Alzheimer's and schizophrenia to the abstraction of intelligence and the weight of depression. First, we understood. Then, we acted. We began to modulate baseline intelligence. We progressed to designing novel neural networks that introduced functions previously unknown to humanity. The final step was clear: we had moved from a biological revolution to evolution.
