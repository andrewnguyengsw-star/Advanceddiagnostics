Our Technology

Cognitive enhancement research is powered by advanced technology that traces neural impulses and translates their encoded messages. This provides an unprecedented view into thought mechanics.

By correlating IQ test outcomes with neural activity, we uncover key neural circuits that differentiate high and low intellectual function. These insights allow precise intervention targeting.

Our Method

We model brain activity of test subjects relative to their performance, tracing neuron activity across the entire test. Observing these changes across space and time allows us to infer the neuroanatomical basis of intelligence.
